MYSTIQ

MYSTIQ is a child wholesale clothing company under Bong Hwa USA Inc. MYSTIQ provides a variety of different clothing from accessories (such as hats) to everyday clothing (such as shirts).

OBJECTIVE

MYSTIQ provides its business partners with catalogs for each upcoming season. They also provide samples and displays whenever a business is interested. MYSTIQ (as well as the other child wholesale companies under Bong Hwa USA Inc.) regularly attend fashion shows where they set up a booth and provide a more personal view of the catalogs and merchandise. MYSTIQ needed a catalogs page where customers can view the catalogs whenever and wherever they are. The only requirement given was to keep it simple as to not confuse the visitors. It was to strictly be a catalogs viewing site, not an online shop.
