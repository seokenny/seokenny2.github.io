ABOUT THE COMPANY

Fuyu Ramen is a new ramen shop that opened up in Sunnyside, Queens on November 2018. It serves different types of ramen as well as appetizers and other Japanese and Korean entrees. The restaurant has a very sleek and modern interior.

OBJECTIVE

Fuyu Ramen's website was extremely unfinished, bare bone, and only accessible in Korean. Since Sunnyside is an upcoming area, I offered to completely redesign the website.
