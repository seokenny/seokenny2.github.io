MY ROLE

At Rugs USA, my main role was to create, maintain, and redesign various new/old web pages and content. I worked mostly with the web development team, but also worked together with the design team to create new content and designs for both the Rugs USA website and for any Rugs USA social media accounts.


Rugs USA was in need of a guide for new and current customers. Some customers would hold off on purchasing a rug due to uncertainty on the different varieties for each rug. The CEO requested a "Rugs 101" guide to provide customers with details and visual information of the different rugs, sizes, and etc. I worked with the design team to create new images for simple references and then designed the mockup for the different pages. These guides would also be available when looking at a certain rug.

The Rugs USA main search page was severely outdated and needed a new design. I was tasked with redesigning the search page and adding new informational content. The "Quick Shop" and "Similar Styles" tab was also to be added under each rug.
