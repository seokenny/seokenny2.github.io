Fuyu Ramen is an authentic Japanese ramen restaurant located in the heart of Sunnyside, Queens NY. We serve Japanese and Korean rice dishes as well as a variety of appetizers from different regions of Asia such as Vietnamese and Thai. Fuyu Ramen uses long simmered pork, chicken, and vegetarian broth for their ramen and fresh noodles for each ramen dish.
